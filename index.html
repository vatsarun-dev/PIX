Pixar Animation Studios

Pixar Animation Studios, a wholly owned subsidiary of The Walt Disney Company, is an Academy Award®-winning film studio with world-renowned technical, creative and production capabilities in the art of computer animation. The Northern California studio has created some of the most successful and beloved animated films of all time, including "Toy Story," "Monsters, Inc.," "Cars," "The Incredibles," "Ratatouille," "WALL•E," "Up," "Brave" and "Inside Out." Its movies have won 37 Academy Awards® and have grossed more than $14 billion at the worldwide box office to date.

CAREERS AT PIXAR

At Pixar, we seek to hire the most talented artists, animators, technicians, and support personnel. We hire people who are passionate about what they do, always inspired to push beyond boundaries, be always learning and curious and be great people to work with.

We believe it's important that our workforce reflects the breadth of the human experience, and we actively pursue diversity throughout our organization. New ideas and perspectives are important to our creative culture. Come see what we have to offer.
